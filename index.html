Event Creation & Management

CultureFinder allows users to easily create, save and manage their upcoming events and experiences. Users can include event details, images, promotions, ticketing information and schedules for all of their events entered in the database. The Events module ensures that every detail can be captured and shared with the public.

Event Data & Analytics

Harnessing data from Google Analytics, CultureFinder Analytics allows system administrators to view and export system-wide event data from all registered organizations. Users also have an analytics view that allows them to see data such as page views and ticket clicks on specified websites for each of their events in CultureFinder.

Event Sharing & Distribution

CultureFinder allows you to easily share or distribute the events entered into the database with other partners, websites, event feeds, publications and more. Event data can be customized and curated to share with third parties and their particular audiences.

Artist Directory Module

The Artist Directory module gives local artists or arts groups a platform to manage their own profile, display their work and receive inquiries from the public through our secure contact form. Artists may choose from a variety of arts disciplines and upload images, videos, audio, and document files to their portfolio. System administrators may review and approve artist profiles before they go live to the Artist Directory, giving you control of the site content.

Public Art Directory

The Public Art Directory is a comprehensive and user-friendly database with searchable and shareable data about public art in your neighborhood, city, region, or state. This module helps capture the full catalog of your community's public art pieces and makes it accessible to members of the public.

Import existing public art information into the database, or ask for the public's help by submitting what they know about pieces of art. System administrators have the ability to review, approve and decline public submissions, giving you control of the content and accuracy of the database.

Our Story

Culture Finder began in 1999 at the dawn of the modern web. The first Centralized Cultural Events Database of its kind allowed marketers from Arts and Cultural organizations around the State of Ohio to publish information about events for syndication and promotion by both online and offline outlets. What started as a basic Events calendar has become one of the most robust technology platforms of its kind.
